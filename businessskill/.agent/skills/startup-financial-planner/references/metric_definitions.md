# Metric Definitions

## MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
MRR = Paying Users × ARPU

## ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
ARR = MRR × 12

## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
Total marketing + sales cost / new customers

## LTV (Lifetime Value)
LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin / Churn Rate

## Churn Rate
% of users lost per month

## Burn Rate
Monthly expenses - revenue

## Runway
Runway = Cash / Burn Rate

## LTV:CAC Ratio
Healthy startup: 3:1 or higher
