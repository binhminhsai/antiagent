Nowadays, more and more women decide to have children later in their life. What are the reasons for this?
Do the advantages of this outweigh disadvantages?

In recent days, the tendency of women making the decision to delay their motherhood is noticeably increasing. This trend might be affected by multiple factors, which will be analyzed in the essay below, bringing about not only notable benefits, but also certain drawbacks that are worth considering. 
There are two crucial advantages of having children in women’s later stage of age. One of them should be acknowledged is that they are emotionally mature and well-qualified enough, both physically and mentally, to step in a broad new milestone of life, which will significantly support them in understanding their children’s psychological patterns remarkably better. Besides, they should have been financially sustainable since they have been working and saving to provide themselves and their beloved people with a decent and comfortable life. As women possess such sustainability, their children are well-supplied with a more well-rounded life experience and development. Maintaining this standard of preparedness may considerably lead to healthier and firmer parent-child relationships in the long-term period of time. 
Looking on the downside, there are certain disadvantages of giving birth later in women’s life. The most striking one could be taken into serious consideration is that fertility is critically undermined. On the grounds that the older a woman is, the worse their health and ability of giving birth are, this risk may cause gynecological diseases in the mother and genealogical disorders in the child. In addition, women living in Asian countries might be forced to be faced with intense societal pressure due to the traditional stereotypes and culture. Having caused these vital consequences, motherhood ought to be seen and taken more appropriate actions towards this landmark within an individual's point of view of this aspect. 
In conclusion, although postponing motherhood allows women to be more experienced and well-prepared, having children late will likely to cause unexpected health-related risks and receive social judgments influencing personal life, which is not recommended as a sustain plan afterwards.
