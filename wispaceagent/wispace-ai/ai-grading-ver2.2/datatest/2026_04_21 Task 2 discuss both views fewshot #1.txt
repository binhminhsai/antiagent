Some people believe that a crime is a result of social problems and poverty, others think that crime is a result of a bad person’s nature. Discuss both views and give your opinion

Public opinions are divided as to whether crime is due to social problems and poverty or bad personality. Personally, both of them are reasonable, but I am inclined to advocate the former than the latter due to some reasons which will be explained in the essay below. 
On the one hand, there are two compelling reasons why a bad person’s nature leads to crime. The most striking one is that hot-tempered individuals tend to exhibit brutal behaviors. Instead of verbal conflict resolution, they often settle disputes with fists and in the worst-case scenario, this negative nature can give rise to domestic violence. Coupled with this is that the risk of theft or pickpocketing is commonly found among those with express selfish tendencies. Surrounded by a large crowd, they desire to exploit others for personal gain such as thefting people’s money or belongings . There is a strong probability to result in embezzlement when it comes to public servants with a greedy disposition. 
On the other hand, I believe that social issues and poverty cause crime for the following reasons. To begin with, children in poor conditions tend to exhibit violent behaviors on the grounds that the more dysfunctional their family became, the more such young individuals were preoccupied with violent behaviors. This regular exposure make them prone to embrace these tendencies recklessly, which is very likely to commit assault in the near future. In addition to this, those who are surrounded by toxic social circles are easily associated with criminal activities. Their peer group may lure them into gambling or betting, rendering them vulnerable to these criminal addiction. 
In conclusion , although it is justifiable for some to endorse the perspective of crime being a result of a negative of negative personality, I firmly maintain that crime being by virtue of social problems and poverty represents a more compelling standpoint.
